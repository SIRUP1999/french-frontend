🇫🇷 About French Oral Master

What does it do?

French Oral Master uses AI (Groq Whisper + Gemini) to transcribe any French oral exam audio and instantly generate a complete 5-section study guide:

  • Word-for-word English translation
  • Key vocabulary with English meanings
  • Grammar points from the audio
  • Sample exam questions with model answers
  • Tips to ace the topic

Quick Tips for Your French Oral Exam

🎧
Listen 3 times
First for meaning, second for detail, third while reading the transcription.
🗣️
Speak out loud
Answer sample questions aloud. Even 5 minutes daily builds fluency fast.
📝
Key phrases
"À mon avis", "Par exemple", "En conclusion" — memorise these transitions.
⏱️
Time yourself
Practice answering within 1–2 minutes to build exam-day conciseness.

Useful French Phrases for Oral Exams
