📖 How to Upload Your Audio Files
Step 1 — Tap the upload box
Tap anywhere on the 🎧 upload area. Your phone's file picker will open.
Step 2 — Find your audio files
Android: Tap ☰ menu → "Files" or "Browse" · iPhone: Tap "Browse" → Downloads or WhatsApp folder
From WhatsApp: Internal Storage → WhatsApp → Media → WhatsApp Audio
Step 3 — Select multiple files
Mobile: Long press first file → tap each remaining → tap Open
Desktop: Hold Ctrl (Windows) or Cmd (Mac) to select multiple files
✅ Tap "Transcribe & Study All"
Each audio gets transcribed with a full study guide automatically.
