Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2026  ·  French Oral Master  ·  Ghana

This Privacy Policy explains how French Oral Master collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website. By using this app, you agree to the terms described below.

1. Who We Are

French Oral Master is a free AI-powered web application that helps students transcribe and study French oral exam audio files. The service is operated from Ghana.

If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us at: frenchoralmaster@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • Audio files — Files you upload are sent to our AI service (Groq) for transcription only. They are not stored on our servers.
  • IP address — Used only to enforce the daily free usage limit (7 transcriptions per day). Not stored permanently.
  • Usage data — We count the number of visits and transcriptions for internal analytics. No personal details are stored.
  • Cookies — We use cookies through Google AdSense and Google Analytics to serve ads and understand site traffic.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI transcription and study guide services
  • To enforce fair daily usage limits
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • To understand how many people use the app (anonymous analytics only)
  • To improve the service over time

4. Audio Files & Your Data

Your uploaded audio files are:

  • Sent securely to Groq AI for transcription
  • Not stored on our servers after processing
  • Not shared with any third party other than Groq for transcription purposes
  • Processed in real time and immediately discarded

You can read Groq's privacy policy at groq.com/privacy-policy

5. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this site. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it to serve ads based on your visits to our site and other sites on the Internet
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings
  • You can also opt out at aboutads.info

For more information on how Google uses data, visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

6. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This collects anonymous data such as pages visited, time spent, and device type. No personally identifiable information is collected.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

7. Cookies

This site uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Google AdSense — for displaying relevant ads
  • Google Analytics — for anonymous traffic analysis

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect how ads are displayed but will not affect the core functionality of the app.

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate this app:

  • Groq AI — for audio transcription and study guide generation (their privacy policy)
  • Google AdSense — for advertisements (Google's privacy policy)
  • Netlify — for website hosting (Netlify's privacy policy)
  • Render — for backend server hosting (Render's privacy policy)

9. Children's Privacy

French Oral Master is intended for students and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

10. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. Audio files are transmitted securely over HTTPS and are not stored after processing. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
